# Inputs Required: Dörfler AG

**Date:** 2026-02-18
**Owner:** Web Agent
**Purpose:** Concrete checklist of what we need from the customer to build the website.

---

## Minimum Viable Inputs (to ship MVP)

These are required to launch. Without them, delivery is blocked.

### Brand
| Item | Why needed | Acceptable formats | Minimum |
|---|---|---|---|
| Logo | Hero, nav, favicon | SVG (preferred), PNG (min 512px, transparent bg) | 1 file |
| Primary brand color | Buttons, accents, CTA | Hex code (e.g., #1A3B5C) | 1 color |
| Company legal name | Footer, Impressum | Plain text | 1 line |

### Content
| Item | Why needed | Acceptable formats | Minimum |
|---|---|---|---|
| Company tagline / promise | Hero H1 headline | Plain text, max 8 words | 1 line |
| Services offered | Service tiles section | Bullet list (service name + 1-line description) | 3 services |
| Service area (regions/PLZ) | Service Area section | List of PLZ codes, city names, or region name | 1 region |
| Notrufnummer (emergency phone) | Notfall CTA + Contact section | Phone number in E.164 format (+41...) | 1 number |
| Office address | Contact section, footer | Street + PLZ + City | 1 address |
| Email for case notifications | Email routing (where cases are sent) | Email address | 1 address |

### Proof / Reviews
| Item | Why needed | Acceptable formats | Minimum |
|---|---|---|---|
| Customer testimonials | Reviews section | Text quote + first name + city (e.g., "M. Müller, Zürich") | 2 reviews |
| Years of experience | Proof pill ("XX Jahre Erfahrung") | Number | 1 value |

### Ops / Email Routing
| Item | Why needed | Acceptable formats | Minimum |
|---|---|---|---|
| Recipient email for cases | Resend sends case notifications here | Email address | 1 address |
| Preferred "From" display name | Email sender name (e.g., "FlowSight für Dörfler AG") | Plain text | 1 line |

## Upgrade to Truly High-End

These are not blockers but make the difference between "functional" and "premium."

### Brand (upgrade)
| Item | Why needed | Acceptable formats | Minimum |
|---|---|---|---|
| Secondary accent color | Visual depth, hover states | Hex code | 1 color |
| Brand fonts (if any) | Typography consistency with print/existing materials | Font name or file (OTF/TTF/WOFF2) | 1–2 fonts |
| Favicon / app icon | Browser tab, mobile bookmark | PNG 512x512 or SVG | 1 file |

### Content (upgrade)
| Item | Why needed | Acceptable formats | Minimum |
|---|---|---|---|
| Detailed service descriptions | Expanded /leistungen subpage (post-MVP) | 3–5 sentences per service | 3 services |
| "About us" company text | About/Team section — builds trust | 3–5 sentences about history, values, team | 1 paragraph |
| Process description (custom) | Process section — if their workflow differs from default | 3–4 steps, plain text | 1 list |

### Photos (upgrade)
| Item | Why needed | Acceptable formats | Minimum |
|---|---|---|---|
| Team photo | About/Team section — real people build trust | JPG/PNG, min 1200px wide, real (not stock) | 1 photo |
| Work-in-progress photos | Hero background, service tiles, gallery | JPG/PNG, min 1200px wide, real on-site work | 3–5 photos |
| Company vehicle / branding | Proof of local presence | JPG/PNG | 1 photo |

### Proof / Reviews (upgrade)
| Item | Why needed | Acceptable formats | Minimum |
|---|---|---|---|
| Additional testimonials | Stronger social proof | Text quote + name + city | 3–5 total |
| Certifications / memberships | Proof pills (e.g., "Suissetec Mitglied") | Name + optional logo (SVG/PNG) | 1–3 items |
| Google Reviews link | External proof reference | URL | 1 link |

### Service Area (upgrade)
| Item | Why needed | Acceptable formats | Minimum |
|---|---|---|---|
| Full PLZ coverage list | Precise service area display | List of PLZ codes or city names | Complete list |
| Response time by area | "Wir sind in XX Minuten bei Ihnen" | Minutes per region/zone | 1–3 zones |

## How to Provide Inputs

1. **Text items:** Add directly to `docs/customers/doerfler-ag/inputs.md` or send as message.
2. **Images:** Place in a shared folder (Google Drive, Dropbox) and share the link — or commit to `docs/customers/doerfler-ag/assets/` (no secrets, no credentials in filenames).
3. **Links:** Add to `docs/customers/doerfler-ag/links.md`.

**Priority:** Start with the "Minimum Viable" table. We can ship with just those items. The "Upgrade" items can follow in a second pass.
