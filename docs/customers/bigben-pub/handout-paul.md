## What you're looking at (top to bottom)

### 2. Hero Image
- Full-screen photo of the pub interior

### 3. "Best Guinness on the Lake" Banner
- Dark banner right below the hero — makes your USP impossible to miss

### 4. About Us
- Short text introducing the pub + your Google stats (4.7 stars, 186+ reviews, 40+ seats)

### 5. What's On — Events
This is the most important section for attracting visitors.

**3 main events with recurring dates:**
- Karaoke Night — **Every Friday**
- Quiz Night — **Every Wednesday**
- Live Music — **Every Saturday**

Plus 4 smaller features: Live Sport, Darts, Private Events, Terrace.

> **What you need to tell us:** Are these days correct? If the schedule changes (e.g. Karaoke moves to Thursday), just send a WhatsApp and we update it same day.

### 6. Food & Drinks (Menu)
- Left card: Pub Bites (8 items)
- Right card: From the Bar (On Tap + Bottles & More)

> **Note:** We wrote "Ask at the bar for today's specials" since there's no printed menu. If you ever create one, we can add prices.

### 7. Google Reviews
- 6 real 5-star reviews from your Google profile
- Gold stars, reviewer names, "Local Guide" badges where applicable
- Shows "4.7 — 186+ Reviews on Google" at the top

> **Why this matters:** Social proof. New visitors trust other guests more than any ad.

### 8. Gallery
- 8 photos: terrace, interior, bar, Guinness, food, darts, cocktails
- We can swap photos anytime — just send new ones via WhatsApp

### 9. Table Reservation (the key feature)
- Guests fill in: Name, Phone, Date, Time, Party Size, Special Requests
- When they hit "Reserve Table":
  1. **Guest gets an SMS** confirming date, time, guests, address
  2. **You (Paul) get an SMS** with all booking details + guest phone number

**What you need to watch:**
- Check your phone for incoming reservation SMS (sender: "BigBenPub")
- If someone books for 9+ guests, they see "call us" — no SMS, they call you
- You do NOT need to log in anywhere. It's all via SMS.
- If you're fully booked on a date, there's no automatic blocking — you'd call the guest to reschedule

**What you do NOT need to worry about:**
- No app to install
- No password to remember
- No dashboard to check
- We handle all the tech — you just read your SMS

### 10. Opening Hours & Map
- Hours displayed per day (Monday closed, Tue-Thu 16-23, Fri-Sat 16-00, Sun 16-22)
- Google Maps embed showing your location
- Instagram link again at the bottom

### 11. Footer
- Address, Instagram, "Website by FlowSight"
