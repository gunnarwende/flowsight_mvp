# Style DNA — Binding Design Policy

## Positioning
Premium, calm, trustworthy, Swiss craftsmanship. The website must feel like a top-tier local business — not a template, not a startup.

## Typography Hierarchy
- **H1:** One per page. Large, bold, short (max ~8 words). Sets the promise.
- **H2:** Section headers. Clear, scannable. Medium weight.
- **Body:** 16–18px base. Comfortable line-height (1.5–1.6). Max ~70ch per line.
- **Spacing:** Generous whitespace between sections. Let content breathe. Minimum 64px vertical section padding.

## Grid Discipline
- Content max-width: ~1200px, centered.
- Consistent horizontal padding (16–24px mobile, 32–64px desktop).
- Align elements to a visible rhythm — no random offsets.

## Component Patterns
- **Hero:** Full-width, strong headline, single CTA, optional background image (real, not stock).
- **Proof Pills:** Minimal trust indicators (e.g., "24h Notdienst", "Lokaler Betrieb"). Max 3–4. No badge spam.
- **Service Tiles:** Clean cards, icon + short title + 1-line description. Consistent sizing.
- **Reviews:** Real names or initials + location. No star-rating spam. 2–3 featured, not a wall.
- **CTA:** One primary action per viewport. Repeat Wizard CTA at natural scroll points. Button, not link.

## Image Style
- Real work photos, real team, real tools. Warm lighting preferred.
- No cheesy stock photos (no handshakes, no fake smiles, no suits).
- Icons: simple, consistent line style. Use sparingly for service tiles and features.

## Copy Tone
- Short sentences. Specific, not generic.
- No fluff words ("innovative", "revolutionär", "einzigartig") — say what you do.
- Use domain language: "Rohrbruch", "Warmwasser", "Heizung Ausfall", "Notfall".
- Urgency vocabulary: "Notfall" / "Dringend" / "Normal" — match case contract.
- Address the customer directly ("Sie haben ein Problem? Wir sind da.")

## No-Go List
- Cheap trust badges or certification logos crammed together
- More than 3 accent colors
- Loud gradients or neon highlights
- Dense text blocks without whitespace
- Gimmicky animations, parallax overload, auto-playing video
- Generic hero images (city skylines, abstract shapes)

## Decision Rule
When in doubt between options: prefer clarity + premium restraint over more content or more features.
